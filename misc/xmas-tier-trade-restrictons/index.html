COVID-19 Tier Restrictions

Christmas Day and New Years Eve

Current COVID-19 Tier hospitality restrictions are putting particular pressure on our little village pub due to its limited availability of dining tables.

In an attempt to attain viable trade, balanced with customer safety regulations, we have been forced to implement the following provisional dining-in terms:

Sittings

From 5.00pm must be booked for either food or drinks; tables are bookable in two-hour slots with the main food services operating two sittings.
Sitting #1 5.30pm – 7.30pm
Sitting #2 7.30pm – close (last food service at 8.30pm)

Booking Fee

Due to limited tables there is a table booking fee of £10.00 per person, minimum charge of £40.00. This table booking fee is non-refundable but can be redeemed against the bill on the night of booking.

Booking terms & conditions will be explained at the time of booking. However, if you have any questions please call Paula on 07912 868111

We apologise for any inconvenience this unusual approach may cause. However, given the acute financial pressures of the current extraordinary trade restrictions levied against small hospitality business’ like ourselves; with our limiting dining tables, we have no choice but employ these interim measures in order to best balance the interests of the business, staff and customers.
