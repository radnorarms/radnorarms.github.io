COVID-19 Tier Restrictions

Current COVID-19 Tier hospitality restrictions are putting particular pressure on our little village pub due to its limited availability of dining tables.

In an attempt to attain viable trade, balanced with customer safety regulations, we have been forced to implement the following provisional dining-in terms:

Sittings

Tables are normally bookable in two-hour slots with the main food services occasionally operating two sittings.

Booking Fee

During some busy evening services there may be a non-returnable reservation fee payable when you book. This fee is fully redeemable on the night against your food and drink bill.

Booking terms & conditions will be explained at the time of booking. However, if you have any questions please call Paula on 07912 868111

We apologise for any inconvenience this unusual approach may cause.
