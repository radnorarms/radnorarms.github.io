The Radnor Arms is a 400-year-old, privately owned, family run village Inn offering a friendly traditional feel with log burners, a beer garden and a wide selection of wines, beers, ciders, ales, spirits and soft drinks. We serve lunch and evening meals and strive to provide a good “Pub Food” service with a varied menu to suit all occasions.

You can find us in the village of Corston near Malmesbury, a little over 3 miles from Junction 17 of the M4. We are close to other popular places such as Cirencester, Westonbirt Arboretum and Tetbury.
