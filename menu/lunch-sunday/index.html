Sunday Roasts

All Sunday Roasts use local butcher sourced meats and are served with roast potatoes, roast parsnips, homemade Yorkshire Pudding, sage & onion stuffing, cauliflower cheese, and a selection of seasonal vegetables.

All Roasts are normally served with our rich “homemade stock gravy”. However, we offer as an alternative a non stock “gluten free meat gravy” and a “vegetarian gravy” option. If you require one of these “alternative” gravy options please state when ordering.
