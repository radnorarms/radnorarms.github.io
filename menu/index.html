Our foods are a mixture of home-prepared, homemade, family and locally sourced produce—to ensure the highest quality—all food is home-cooked to order.

We have an extensive menu choice with regularly changing Chef’s Specials.

We are passionate about the quality and standard of our food, with our own rich stock-based sauces and gravies. We also provide a wide range of vegetarian, pasta, fish, poultry, meat and curry dishes—as well as traditional pub style food.

**The prices quoted in each menu were correct at the time of publishing; however, they are subject to change without notice. Please check before booking /ordering.

**Please note that we use nuts in some of our dishes, therefore some of our food may contain traces of nuts.
