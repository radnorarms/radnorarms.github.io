Sunday Takeaway Menu

All Sunday Roasts come with roast potatoes, roast parsnips, homemade Yorkshire Pudding, sage & onion stuffing, cauliflower cheese, and a selection of vegetables.

All Roasts are normally served with our rich “homemade stock gravy”. However, we also offer as an alternative a non stock “gluten free meat gravy” and a “vegetarian gravy” option. If you require one of these “alternative” gravy options please state when ordering.

Ordering is easy

Pre-order only to 01666 823389 or 07912 868111. Payment required at point of order by card.

Due to these difficult times we can’t guarantee all stock will be available, however we will do our best.

**For allergy information please ask a Manager. Also, please note nuts and peanuts are used in our kitchen.
