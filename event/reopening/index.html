Re-opening

Hopefully, we will be re-opening as the lockdown will be over, all with the COVID-19 government guidelines being properly observed.

Please come and see us; we would love to welcome you to our family business. Note that there is limited space inside so we recommend you book in advance—however, there are plenty of tables outside—some of which are under cover.

In the meantime, we are providing a takeaway service until the lockdown ends. You can choose from the weekday menu, or the Sunday menu on Sundays.
