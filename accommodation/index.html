The rooms are set within the court yard of The Radnor Arms and are accessed via the private hall area using an electronic fob system. The double room is furnished with a king-size bed that can be split into singles with advance notice. Tea and coffee making facilities are available, free WiFi and Smart TV. The en- suite bathroom has a full mirrored wall, shower, sink/vanity unit, WC and heated towel rail. The Single room is compact and cosy with a spacious ensuite, ideal for an overnight stay.
